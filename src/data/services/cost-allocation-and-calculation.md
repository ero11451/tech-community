### Connect. Collaborate. Create.

**Nivolia Connect 2025** is where bold ideas meet bright minds.  
Join us in **London** (or online) for an evening that blends connection, creativity, and collaboration — designed for founders, builders, and dreamers shaping the next wave of innovation.


### What to Expect

- **Lightning Talks** — Hear from startup founders, investors, and innovators in 7-minute high-impact talks.  
- **Roundtable Discussions** — Join interactive breakout groups focused on scaling, funding, and leadership.  
- **Partner Showcase** — Meet our ecosystem partners supporting startups and emerging tech.  
- **Networking Sessions** — Curated connections that lead to meaningful collaborations.

### Who Should Attend

- Entrepreneurs and startup founders  
- Developers, designers, and product managers  
- Investors, accelerators, and mentors  
- Anyone looking to connect and grow within the innovation community

### Event Details

- **Date:** Friday, December 12, 2025  
- **Time:** 18:00 – 21:30 (GMT)  
- **Location:** The Loft @ Innovation Hub, London (Hybrid — in-person + livestream)  
- **Tickets:** £15 (in-person) / Free (virtual)  
- **Register:** [Join the Event](https://nivolia.com/events/nivolia-connect-2025)


### Why Attend

- **Genuine Connections** – Meet people who can help you grow and collaborate.  
- **Actionable Insights** – Real stories, tools, and strategies from founders and industry experts.  
- **Inspiration & Energy** – Leave with ideas, motivation, and contacts that matter.  
- **Community Spirit** – Experience the power of the Nivolia Network in action.  



### FAQ

**Do I need to be a Nivolia member to attend?**  
No! Everyone is welcome. Members simply get early access and special perks.

**Can I attend virtually?**  
Yes — the event will be livestreamed and all sessions recorded for replay.

**Can I speak or host a session?**  
Absolutely. Submit your topic by November 24 at [speak@nivolia.com](mailto:speak@nivolia.com).

**How can I sponsor or partner with Nivolia?**  
We love collaborating. Email [partnerships@nivolia.com](mailto:partnerships@nivolia.com) for sponsorship details.

**Are tickets refundable?**  
Yes — up to 7 days before the event. You can also transfer your ticket to someone else for free.
